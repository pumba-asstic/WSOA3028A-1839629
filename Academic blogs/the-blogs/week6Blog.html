UX has different effects and laws that it comes with. here are the following Laws and effect of the UX

  1. the VON Restorff effect: it is having similar objects, that are presented and one that differes from the rest that is mostly likey to be remembered
  2. HICK's Law: it is the time that it takes to make a decision that increases with the numebr and complexity of choices
  3. Fitt's law: the time to acqure a target is a function fof the distance and the size of the target
  4. Zeigarnik effect: it refers to how people remember the uncompleted or interrupted tasks better than completed tasks
  5. Serial Position Effect:it refers to users having a propensity to best remember the first and the last items on the series.
this shows how a good UX can effect the way that users engage or disengage with your website. these effect will help in designing of the website and how you want the users to engage with the content that is displayed.
